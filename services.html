Workshops

Greg Donaldson’s police communications workshops are designed to enhance department and individual officers’ ability to communicate effectively with members of the community and to develop police supervisors leadership skills and communications strategies. 

1

Power and Persuasion

Public speaking challenges for police officers. Whether it's as simple as speaking at roll call or as stressful as standing before television cameras, this workshop will teach how to form your message and deliver it with polish.

2

Stand and Deliver

Explaining crime strategies to both police administrators and community. This workshop will teach how to adapt to varied audience and deliver a message powerful and cogent.

3

Feet to the Fire

How to survive and thrive at CompStat. Painting a picture the bosses will understand. Knowing the difference between the overall theme and the details of your story makes all the difference. How to think on your feet and tell your story at the podium.

Private Tutoring

Private tutoring in police communications is also available. Upon request, individual supervisors can schedule Donaldson for one-on-one sessions and/or have him attend their particular speaking engagements and give feedback.
